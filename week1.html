Week 1 — Project Concept & Vision

Posted by Team • January 2026

This week, our team focused on establishing a clear concept and vision for our game, AstrOh-No! We aimed to define the core gameplay mechanics, art style, and overall narrative direction.

What We Worked On

We conducted brainstorming sessions to outline the game's key features and themes. The team created mood boards to visualize the art style and began drafting the narrative framework.

Challenges

One challenge we faced was aligning everyone's vision for the game. To address this, we scheduled additional meetings to ensure all team members could contribute their ideas and feedback.

Next Steps

Outline goals for next week. We plan to start prototyping the core mechanics and establishing the visual style.
